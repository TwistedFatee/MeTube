MeTube Rules

Policies and Safety
  1. When you use MeTube, you join a community of people from all over the world.
  2. Every cool, new community feature on MeTube involves a certain level of trust.
  3. Millions of users respect that trust and we trust you to be responsible too.
  4. Following the guidelines below helps to keep MeTube fun and enjoyable for everyone.

Community Guidelines
  1. Don't post videos that encourage others to do things that might cause them to get badly hurt, especially kids.
  2. It's not okay to post violent or gory content that's primarily intended to be shocking, sensational, or gratuitous.
  3. Everyone hates spam. Don't create misleading descriptions, tags, titles, or thumbnails in order to increase views.
